A Brief Introduction to HotBeans

HotBeans is a website development company based in London in the United Kingdom, we take commissions from customers and if we agree on a contract we design and build a full website from the ground up. So far HotBeans has shown great success in the time we have been operational with the almost one hundred website contracts we have been entrusted to build for our customers demonstrating that we are a capable company and show great promise for our future in website development. HotBeans is a fairly small scale company run by a close knit team of website developers and designers creating a very creative atmosphere amongst the workplace resulting in end products that truly show this level of creativity and team work.

How Much is a Contract

As we said we take contracts of all sizes from all customers so it really depends on alot of factors such as: amount of web pages, amount of code, how complex the idea sounds, how much media has to be handled, how much media has to be created, how long it takes and a couple other factors that can be discussed if you come to us with a proposal. The process of purchasing a build contract goes as follows: a customer comes into contact with us about an idea, we discuss the idea with them to understand their idea fully, if they are fully serious about purchasing then the entire HotBeans team will then discuss the idea briefly and come up with a rough figure for what it will cost and time frame and then the customer can decide if they would like to go through with it, if they do then we start prototyping things and making basic designs looking into how their designs will work which will allow us to get a final accurate cost which we can then send to the client, after payment has been recieved we can then start working full speed ahead on their project.
