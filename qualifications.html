 Qualifications

The qualifications and courses shown on this page are not necessarily required in order to become a web developer with HotBeans, however these qualifications are specifically recognised as stand out certifications.


The following are also not in any particualr order of desirability or skill level.

Systems Infrastructure Level 3 Diploma

A level 3 diploma is a good qualification for a systems architect wanting to get a good start into leanring how to build networks and servers.

Website Developer Level 5 Diploma

A level 5 diploma in website development is a great gateway qualification into a website developer career.

Google UX Design Certificate

The Google UX design certificate is the perfect certification for someone wanting to get into UX/UI design and requiring a qualification from a recognised group.

GitLab Certifications

GitLab offers a range of certifications from their technical exams which are constructed in order to prove that the person holding the certificate has a considerable level of knowledge in that field.

Azure DevOps Certifications

Azure DevOps is a similar scheme to the GitLab certifications, it is a Microsoft run programme which offers a multitude of exams proving to an employer or anyone looking upon you that you have a significant level of knowledge in the field you took the examination in.

AWS Certifications

AWS certifications are also similar to GitLab and Azure DevOps, run by AWS(Amazon Web Services) they offer another huge range of course and exams which will demonstrate extensive knowledge in that field.

Architecting Google Compute Engine Specialization

Architecting with google compute engine specialization is a professional certificate offered by Google Cloud there to prove that you are experienced and knowledgeable in cloud-based architecturing.
